AUDITORIUM

Auditorium is the final project created at The Iron Yard. Created with a Rails Engineer, Auditorium is a real time video conferencing application using WebRTC standards. With Auditorium, you can create profiles, moderate lectures, events, & presentations, or participate in other events, asking questions or listening in as a viewer.
